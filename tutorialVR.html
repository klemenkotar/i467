Tutorial

Welcome to the web VR tutorial. Read through the features of the tour, then click start at the bottom of the page to launch the HFS Tour in your browser.

Firefox

Web VR is currently only supported by the Firefox web browser. You can download it for free on Mac, Windows or Linux.

Downlaod Firefox

Mandarin? 英文?

After entering the tour you will be prompted by two flags representing your narration language choices. Click on a flag to select the language.

Gaze and Click

Inside the Web VR experience you can use your mouse to gaze around the room. If a door opens, or an animation appears you can click the left mouse button to move to anotheter part of the room.

Have Fun!

Enjoy exploring your future home. As you walk around, a voice narration will provide you with information about the rooms, student life and UW in general. Click the Start button to launch the web app.
