Market Analysis

The impact of virtual tours on decision making when choosing a destination (i.e., college) According to the study done by University of Nairobi, 90% of the participants claimed that the virtual tour made them decide that they want to visit the destination. Of those who completed the online survey, 92% of them claimed that they preferred virtual tours to photos or videos.
