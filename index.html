VR?

Virtual reality is a computer-generated immersive environment that simulates experience through senses and perception, creating an experience not possible in ordinary physical reality.

Release Note

HFS Tour

The HFS tour takes you
on a journey through the newly developed Willow Hall, offering a look inside this exciting north campus community and giving you a feel
for student life at the UW.

Their Problems, Our Solutions

This experience transports
incoming students around the globe from the comfort of their home, to the comfort of their (new) home without the need for expensive international travel and planned visits.

How To Access It

The experience is available
on the Google Cardboard virtual reality platform. If you have received a headset in the mail, now is the time to activate it. You can also purchase one online for five dollars.

No Cardboard? No Problem?

If you do not have access
to Google Cardboard or an Android device, you can still experience this virtual tour from the comfort of your web browser.

About The Project

HFS VR was developed by
a group of students at the University of Washington, with the mission of reducing the physical distances that separate people.
