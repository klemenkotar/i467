Google Cardboard

In order to access HFS VR, you will need to scroll to the bottom of the website where it offers two ways of accessing the product. You can choose to access HFS VR either with Google Cardboard or without. If you want to take the tour with Google Cardboard, you will need to download an android version of the application on the phone by clicking “VR”. Then you will need to open the mobile application, place your phone into Google Cardboard, and start you journey. It is fine if you don’t have Google Cardboard. You can still take the virtual tour simply via the interactive virtual video on the website.
