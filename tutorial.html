Tutorial

Welcome to the VR tutorial. Now is the time to unfold and assemble your cardboard headset and get your Android device. Read through the features of the tour, download the app, insert your phone into the cardboard, and let us bring the building to you!

Mandarin? 英文?

After entering the tour you will be prompted by two flags representing your narration language choices. Look at the flag of your choice and click the button on the side of your headset to select this language.

Follow The Light

We want you to feel right at home, so feel free to look around. Whenever your gaze turns on a light, you can click the button on the side of your headset to explore that location.

Have Fun!

Enjoy exploring your future home. As you walk around, a voice narration will provide you with information about the rooms, student life and UW in general. Click the Start button to download the app.
